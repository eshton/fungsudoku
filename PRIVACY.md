# Fung Sudoku — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 24 April 2026_

Fung Sudoku respects your privacy.

## What we collect

**Nothing.**

- No personal data
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports sent off-device
- No device identifiers
- No cookies, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting
- No IP logging (the app does not make network requests)

## What the app stores on your device

Fung Sudoku keeps three small pieces of data in its sandboxed storage area on your device:

1. **Your saved games** — puzzles in progress and completed, with board state, times, and scores. Stored as a JSON file in the app's Application Support directory.
2. **Your language preference** — which of the 25 supported languages to display.
3. **Your appearance preference** — Light, Dark, or follow the system.

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes it all.

## Network access

Fung Sudoku does **not** connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline. It does not call any server — ours or anyone else's.

## Third parties

The app contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, or telemetry of any kind.

## Children's privacy

Fung Sudoku's Kids mode is intended for children. Because we collect no data of any kind, the app is consistent with COPPA (US), GDPR-K (EU), the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code, and equivalent regulations worldwide. No parental consent flow is needed because there is nothing to consent to.

## Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations, you have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. Since we hold none, these rights have nothing to act on. If you have questions regardless, see the contact below.

## Apple platform data

Some metrics Apple chooses to collect about app installs and crash reports (via the App Store / TestFlight infrastructure) are governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/), not by us. Fung Sudoku itself does not opt into any of Apple's optional telemetry.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Because the app is fully offline, we have no way to notify you directly — please check this page occasionally if that matters to you.
