## Features

- **Classic 9×9** puzzles across four difficulty presets — Easy, Moderate, Hard, Expert
- **Three Beginner tiers** for new solvers working their way up
- **Kids 4×4 mode** with all helpers on — perfect for first-time puzzlers
- **Custom difficulty** — set your own clue count, maximum technique tier, and intensity
- **Live per-tile scoring** — every correct placement earns points on the spot, faster fills score higher
- **Pencil-mark notes** that auto-clear from peer cells when you commit a digit
- **Undo, same-digit highlighting,** and a digit-count tracker in the number pad
- **Gold / Silver / Bronze medals** based on speed and accuracy
- **Stats** tracking best scores, times, and medals per difficulty
- **25 languages** with a built-in language switcher
- **Light, Dark, and System** appearance modes
- **Save and resume** — every game is stored locally

**No ads. No accounts. No tracking. No internet required.**

## Frequently asked

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes. Fung Sudoku does not connect to the internet at all.

**Are there in-app purchases?**
No.

**Are there ads?**
No.

**Do you store my data in the cloud?**
No. Your saved games live only on your device. If you delete the app, they're gone.

**Can I sync across devices?**
Not in this version. Maybe a later release.

**Where did the Sudoku techniques come from?**
The app implements 11 human-style solving techniques (Naked Single through XYZ-Wing). The "How to Play" tab walks through each with interactive diagrams.

**The app says my score dropped — why?**
It didn't. Score is locked once earned; Undo and Erase only move the fill counter, they don't refund points. Placing a wrong digit doesn't score anything either.
